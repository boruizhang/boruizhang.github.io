About Me [resume]

I'm a Ph.D. student in Linguistics (minor in Computer Science) at the Institute of Linguistics at the University of Minnesota, Twin Cities. I’m interested in syntax, semantics, and natural language processing for endangered languages. My current research work is on Kathmandu Newari (Newa, Newar, or Nepal Bhasa). What's Newar language?

Research Activies and Publications

  • Complementation in Newari : Presentation at the seventh Formal Approaches to South Asian Languages (FASAL 7) workshop, March 2017, MIT, Boston
